What is VILDE?

Vilde is a Norse mythology-themed roguelike first-person shooter game. Players take on the role of a warrior who must defeat an evil version of herself who has resurrected all of the foes that the player has defeated in the past. With a dark, epic art style inspired by Norse mythology and Viking culture, players will face challenging combat against enemies in procedurally generated levels filled with traps and obstacles. Players can upgrade their character with persistent upgrades and boons, and make use of a variety of weapons and abilities inspired
by Norse legends.

Features

  • A variety of weapons, modern weapons with Norse mythological abilities
  • Two core slots defining character build with powerful passive and active abilities
  • Boons that empower weapons and core slots with stat upgrades and special effects
  • Incrementally increasing difficulty with each defeated final boss
  • Ability to permanently upgrade character using currency earned from previous play-throughs
  • Norse mythology-inspired locations
  • Dark, epic art style based on Norse gods and monsters
  • Currency earned from previous play-throughs used to upgrade character
  • Stylized art style
